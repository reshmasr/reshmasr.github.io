About Us

RR edusat Study Abroad is an overseas education facilitator, headquartered in Trivandrum, Kerala, India. We follow the path of success and provide comprehensive solutions to students on their journey to a successful academic career in universities across the globe. We assist students to make the right choice with regard to pursuing higher education in high-quality educational institutions abroad.

RR edusat Study abroad is a trusted brand for providing the best service for study abroad aspirants in Kerala and we have carved a niche in helping students 24*7 basis from the profile analysis till the post landing.

Our vision is to be the best in the business and service our customers with the best possible quality and service. Our mission is to achieve the highest level of our customers satisfaction. We value our relationship with the customer and the confidence they have imposed in us in order to provide them with the best possible quality & service. We can never afford to compromise on the discharge of our responsibility while providing our best quality services.
